here's timeline of mohammed salah

  • 1992- Born In Nagrig, Basyoun, Egypt

  • 2010-Professional Debut for El Mokawloon

  • 2010-He scored his first goal for them on 25 December 2010 in a 1–1 away draw against Al Ahly.

  • 2011-On 3 September 2011, Salah made his debut for the Egypt national football team in the 2–1 away defeat by Sierra Leone.

  • 2012-Salah had signed for Basel on a four-year contract

  • 2014-Chelsea announced that a deal had been agreed with Basel to bring Salah to London for a fee reported to be in the region of £11 million.

  • 2015-in JanuaryChelsea confirmed that Salah would play for the Italian club Fiorentina on an 18-month loan until the end of the 2015–16 season

  • 2015-Salah joined Roma on a season-long loan for €5 million; with the option to make the deal permanent, for a reported €15 million.

  • 2017-Salah agreed a transfer to Liverpool. He signed a long-term contract with the Reds for an initial €42m fee that could rise to €50m.

  • 2018-April 2018, Salah was awarded the PFA Players' Player of the Year award, having earlier been named in the PFA Team of the Year for the Premier League and Also Premier League Top Goalscorer.

  • 2018 -in the 2018 UEFA Champions League Final against Real Madrid, Salah injured his left shoulder in the 30th minute. After initially carrying on, he left the field in tears after going to ground in a challenge with Madrid defender Sergio Ramos; the match ended in a 3–1 defeat.

  • 2019- June, he scored Liverpool's first goal in a 2–0 win over Tottenham in the 2019 UEFA Champions League Final from a penalty. Salah's goal, which he scored in the opening two minutes of the match

  • 2020 - Mohamed Salah won the Premier League with Liverpool as the club got their first premier league title and their first league title in 30 years.

  • 2020- in October 2020 Mohamed Salah score 100 goals for Liverpool during Merseyside Derby against Everton in Goodison Park
